    About Us

    Race n Flames is an inclusive, volunteer run community Luge Club that participates in outdoor program and let people race by gravity pull. We designed a large scale, outdoor track, with extremely big sweep bends and curves that's perfcet for speed and thrill, and made sure they were safe and well maintained too.

  • We are lucky to be part of Mt. Buller Resort only Luge Race Club as well as our sponsors for providing us support to be located in such beautiful mountain top and such perfect luge race tracks. We are also very grateful to be allowed a contained and safe space for our participants to enjoy Luge down Summit Right and Howqua trail. Our team's goal is to make sure every single one of our guests enjoy a great time with guaranteed safety precautions for people all ages.
